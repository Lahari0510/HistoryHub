Battle of Plassey (1757)

The battle was fought between the British East India Company and the Nawab of Bengal, Siraj-ud-Daulah. The British victory marked the beginning of their dominance in India.

Revolt of 1857

Also known as the First War of Independence, the revolt was the first major challenge to British rule in India, involving leaders like Rani Lakshmibai, Tantia Tope, and Bahadur Shah Zafar.

Indian Independence (1947)

On August 15, 1947, India gained independence from British rule after a long struggle led by Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, Subhas Chandra Bose, and many others. This marked the end of nearly 200 years of British colonial rule.
